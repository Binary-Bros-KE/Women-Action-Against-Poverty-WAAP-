Our Core Values

Mission

To support marginalized communities in the greater Horn of Africa to sustainably improve their livelihoods through integrated food security, Health and Nutrition, WASH, education, natural resource management, peace building, institutional building and achieve social justice.

Vision

Towards community-based sustainable development through inclusion and social justice where people live in dignity.

Purpose

  • Building healthy communities.
  • Promoting sustainability.
  • Working through partnerships
  • To bring women into full participation.
  • Serving the disadvantaged people.

Our focus

We put women and girls in the center of our work by advancing gender equality, women's empowerment and human rights to overcome poverty.
